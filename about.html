About High Variance

Dec 29th, 2013

During the day, I spend my time teaching and doing research on economics and statistics at Yale, and most evenings and weekends, I’m playing with and taking care of my two wonderful girls. When inspiration strikes and deadlines permit, I write here. My interests are eclectic, but most articles seem to fall into a small number of categories which you see near the top of most pages: kids, music, tech, and economics. You can click on these links and bookmark them separately if you’d like.

You are also welcome to subscribe to the site’s RSS feed, and if you want to follow just some of this blog, you can subscribe the category specific feeds:

  • Kids Feed
  • Music Feed
  • Tech Feed
  • Economics Feed

You can subscribe via email or follow @HighVarianceNet on Twitter to get updated whenever I post something new.

I also blog about teaching and education in general at Teach Better.
