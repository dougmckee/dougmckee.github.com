About Me and This Site

Dec 18th, 2012

During the day, I spend my time teaching and doing research on economics and statistics at Yale, and most evenings and weekends, I’m playing with and taking care of my two wonderful girls. And when inspiration strikes and deadlines permit, I write here. My interests are eclectic, but most articles seem to fall into a small number of categories which you see near the top of most pages: kids, education, music, tech, and economics. You can click on these links and bookmark them separately if you’d like.

You are also welcome to subscribe to the site’s RSS feed, and if you want to follow just some of this blog, you can subscribe the category specific feeds:

  • Kids Feed
  • Education Feed
  • Music Feed
  • Tech Feed
  • Economics Feed

I don’t generally pass on links to good stuff I find in my travels on the web, but I do keep track of some of it publically.
