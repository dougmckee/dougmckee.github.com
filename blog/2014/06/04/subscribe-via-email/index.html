Subscribe via Email

Jun 4th, 2014

I am happy to report that you can now to subscribe to High Variance by email. Just look under the Follow heading on the right side of any page, fill in your email address, and press the Follow this site via email button. Blogtrottr will send you a confirmation message, and then you’ll get every new article emailed to you within an hour of it going live. If you prefer, you can still subscribe to the site’s RSS feed or follow me on Twitter for updates.
